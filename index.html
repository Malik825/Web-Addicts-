WOMEN IN STATISTICS (WIStats) GHANA CELEBRATES ITS OWN ON INTERNATIONAL WOMEN’S DAY Women In Statistics (WIStats) Ghana joins UN women to celebrate International Women’s Day (IWD 2021)on March 8th 2021. The UN Women proposed theme for this year is entitled.

Theme:“Women in leadership: Achieving an equal future in a COVID-19 world.”

WIStats Ghana uses this opportunity to Acknowledge and Appreciate the beautiful brains and hardworking WOMEN (members) behind the progress of the organization (Women In Statistics Ghana). Your leadership, innovation, time and passion to promote statistics and help younger females in their career path is laudable. During the inception of the pandemic, WIStats contributed in terms of Covid-19 sensitization, sharing of handmade nose mask, researches on helping curb the spread of the Covid-19 virus, webinars (online seminars) on covid-19 related issues, among others. May God give us the grace and good health to continue with this good course even us we find innovative ways to live, care for our families and work in this new normal.

WIstats Ghana also wishes to salute all women and frontline workers in their leadership and various efforts to help make this world a safer place for us all.
